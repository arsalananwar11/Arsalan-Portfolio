About Arsalan

Arsalan is a data science enthusiast who is currently working as a Data Scientist in a Martket Intelligence MNC based out of Bangalore, India. He is the founder and researcher at Data Science Crew which is a platform for data science enthusiasts to collaborate and work on exciting AI/ML problems. Arsalan is also an Adjunct Faculty at Intellipaat where he handles few Data Science PGP programs which are in collaboration with MIT and IIT-M.
